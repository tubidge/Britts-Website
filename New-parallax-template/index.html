Bluffdale, UT

Britt's Practice is located at the south end of Salt Lake valley in Bluffdale, UT. She primarily serves clients living in southern Salt Lake county and northern Utah county.

She is conveniently located near the intersection of Redwood Road and Bangerter Highway, easily accessible from I-15.

Experienced

Brittany graduated from Utah College of Massage Therapy in 2013 and has been actively practicing ever since. She has experience working for and managing massage spas, but prefers her work as a solo practitioner in her private practice. This allows her to better develop strong relationships and a deep understanding of each of her client's needs.
